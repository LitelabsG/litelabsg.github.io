About Us
Boutique developer · Privacy-first
▾

Lite Labs is a boutique software developer crafting refined, privacy-respecting digital experiences. As a sole developer studio, we focus on clean, modern, and user-focused applications — engineered without compromise on user privacy.

VTS — Virtual Trading School is our flagship educational application, designed to teach the mechanics of financial markets within a fully virtual, risk-free environment. Every price, chart, news event, balance, and trade displayed within the application is generated locally by algorithmic simulation on your own device. The application does not connect to any real market, exchange, broker, or financial institution. All asset names are fictional symbols chosen purely for educational realism.

Our mission is straightforward: to help learners understand how trading mechanics function — orders, leverage, stop-loss and take-profit logic, position management, and market psychology — before they consider exposing themselves to real financial risk. We believe rigorous education must always precede exposure to capital.

Our privacy commitment: Lite Labs, as a sole developer, operates under a strict privacy-first philosophy. We do not collect personal information of any kind. No accounts are required, no passwords are stored, no contacts or files are accessed, and no advertising networks are integrated. Your progress, settings, and virtual trade history remain on your device at all times.

To help us improve the application over time, we collect anonymous usage analytics only — aggregate measurements such as which features are used and overall session activity. This data is fully anonymized, contains no personally identifying information, and is used solely for product development purposes. You may opt out at any time through your browser settings.

VTS is and will remain completely free of charge. Voluntary contributions through Ko-fi help cover hosting, typography licenses, and ongoing development time. They are entirely optional and unlock no additional features.
