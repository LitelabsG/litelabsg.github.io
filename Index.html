Our Principles

What we stand for

Four guiding principles that shape every product decision we make.

01

Privacy by Design

Privacy is not a feature to be added later — it is the foundation upon which we build. We collect only what is strictly necessary, and never personal information.

02

No Advertising

Our applications contain no advertisements of any kind. We do not participate in advertising networks, behavioral tracking, or interest-based marketing.

03

No Data Harvesting

We do not build user profiles, do not sell data, and do not share information with third parties. What you do on our applications stays on your device.

04

Refined Interfaces

We believe software should be beautiful as well as functional. Every interface is crafted with attention to typography, spacing, and the small details that elevate experience.
