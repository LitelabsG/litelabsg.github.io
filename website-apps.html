VTS — Virtual Trading School

An educational virtual trading platform — learn the mechanics of financial markets in a fully risk-free environment.

VTS is designed for learners who want to understand how trading mechanics function — orders, leverage, stop-loss and take-profit logic, position management, and market psychology — before they consider exposing themselves to real financial risk. Every price, chart, news event, balance, and trade is generated locally by algorithmic simulation on your own device. No real markets, no real brokers, no real money.
